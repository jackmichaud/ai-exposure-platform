# Initial Industries and Occupations

## Scope

MVP covers a curated set of industries and occupations to demonstrate the platform. Selected to span a range of AI exposure levels, wage bands, and education requirements.

## Initial Industries

| Industry | Rationale |
|----------|-----------|
| Healthcare | High augmentation potential, mixed automation risk |
| Finance & Insurance | Significant AI adoption underway |
| Technology | Both creators and subjects of AI disruption |
| Education | Emerging AI integration, public interest |
| Manufacturing | Traditional automation + new AI applications |

## Initial Occupations (3-5 per industry)

### Healthcare
- Registered Nurse — high interpersonal, moderate AI augmentation
- Radiologist — high AI impact on diagnostic tasks
- Medical Coder — high automation risk for coding tasks
- Physical Therapist — low automation, some augmentation

### Finance & Insurance
- Financial Analyst — significant augmentation potential
- Loan Officer — high automation risk for underwriting
- Accountant — mixed automation and augmentation
- Insurance Adjuster — moderate automation risk

### Technology
- Software Developer — strong augmentation, some automation
- Data Scientist — augmentation-heavy
- IT Support Specialist — moderate automation risk
- QA Tester — high automation risk

### Education
- K-12 Teacher — low automation, moderate augmentation
- College Professor — low automation, research augmentation
- Instructional Designer — moderate augmentation potential

### Manufacturing
- Assembly Line Worker — high automation risk
- Quality Control Inspector — high AI augmentation
- Industrial Engineer — moderate augmentation
- CNC Machine Operator — moderate automation risk
